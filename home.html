Google Pixel 9 review: Great cameras, longer battery life

Taking the lead as Google’s big-screen flagship phone, the Google Pixel 9 Pro XL comes packed with lots of new upgrades and AI features in the hopes of taking on the iPhone 16 Pro Max and Galaxy S24 Ultra. With its 6.8-inch Super Actua display, Tensor G4 chip and new 42MP selfie camera, the hardware is being supplemented by new AI features such as Add Me, Gemini Live, Pixel Studio and more. Google Pixel 9 review

The Google Pixel 9 falls into a few of the usual pitfalls for Google's phones, chiefly weak performance. But the Pixel 9 excels in many key areas, with battery life taking a major leap forward. Camera quality is still as impressive as ever — even if the Pixel 9's output doesn’t always compare to more premium rivals. Google continuing its pledge for 7 years of software support means this could easily be a phone you keep around for many years to come.
